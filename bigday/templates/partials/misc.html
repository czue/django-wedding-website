Organic

Organic food means no persistent pesticides or synthetic fertilizers, which might have dangerous consequences on human health; no GMOs with unknown consequences for us and for our ecosystem; and better treatment of animals, including no synthetic growth or breeding hormones. It is a cause that Cory’s parents are extremely passionate about and something we were excited to bring to our wedding.

All of the vines at Ravine Vineyard are organically certified. Ingredients for all food are locally sourced and organically inspired. Bonus: they bake their own bread, raise their own pigs, and grow their own certified organic vegetables!
